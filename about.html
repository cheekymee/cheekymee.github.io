About us

Opened by 3 noodle & food lovers who want to bring a taste of Asia to your doorstep. We hope your cheeks will be full of noodles when you have a bowl of ours, hence “Cheekymee”!

Everything we serve – from noodles, to chilli oil is made from scratch. Our noodles are egg noodles or “mee pok” as we call it in Singapore, and a common dish across coffee shops in the country. The cheekymeelondon team wants to bring a modern taste of Singapore to you!
